Hotel Management System

This program works as an efficient cost as well as time management system. All the details of the occupants are stored into the memory and are accessed using the program. The desired rooms are provided to the occupants on the basis of their requirements. From the time of check in to the time of check out all the expenses  including room service, laundry, game room, chauffer charges, housekeeping  are added to the tab and the final amount will be displayed at the time of check out.
